What is an event?


Currently there are no mechanics backing it up, but an event can spawn resources, or have them be lost. Specific technologys can be targeted, or granted - although not as strong as Advances! It takes an Event to remove an Event.

From the Base Rules: Event - Through this power come the changing of fortunes for an Avatar, Order, City, or Civilization. A gold mine may be found, a storm at sea may strike a fleet, an Avatar may escape death, plague, famine, drought may strike, or technologies may be lost. These are the great, unplanned winds of fortune that blow through the history of the world.
