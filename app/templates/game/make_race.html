Making a race is a serious undertaking, creating a race of sophonts to work your will upon the world. Take care, as others may influence them as well!

Culture: The name of the culture or specific peoples. Examples: French, Sudanese, Navajo.
Race: The name of the species of people. Examples: Humans, Elves, Dwarves.
Subrace: This race splintered off another race instead of being made entirely new. Examples: High Men --> Barbarian Men, High Elves --> Wood Elves, Wolves --> Dogs
Religion: Every race as a cultural set of beliefs through which the Gods can motivate them. They do not have to be a religion by standard meaning, but the term remains the same. Examples: Christianity, Animism, Bhuddism.
